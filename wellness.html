Wellness Exam

A wellness exam is just a routine preventative care. During the exam the physician will focus on wellness of the patient
as well as preventing any health problems that may be found during the exam.




Exam will include:
• Physical examination
• Check of vitals
• Check of BMI
• Shots and immunications if needed
• Develop plan for health
